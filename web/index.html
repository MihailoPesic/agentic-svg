The loop nobody ships

VTracer, Potrace, Illustrator trace once and stop. We close the feedback loop: render → diff → spend shapes where the error is. Same idea as research-grade LIVE, no GPU required.

Spends detail where it matters

Error-targeted refinement puts shapes on the parts that are wrong, not uniformly. A clean logo finishes in a handful of shapes; a gradient photo gets as many as it needs.

Clean, editable, layered

A real traced skeleton underneath, corrections stacked on top — not a blind mesh of polygons. Resolution-independent and ready for print, laser, vinyl, embroidery.
